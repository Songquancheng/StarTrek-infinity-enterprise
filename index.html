Our Mission

Our mission is to explore the unknown universe and open up new homes for humanity. The Mars 99 mission aims to establish the first human colony and offer hope for life in the future. We firmly believe that through space exploration and advanced technology, we can solve the challenges facing the Earth and usher in a new era of human development. Every volunteer will shoulder this great mission, inherit the spirit of the pioneers, and become a bright star in the history of mankind. Join us in this great adventure and create endless possibilities!
